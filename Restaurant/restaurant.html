Our story

The Legendary Restaurant’s roots can be traced all the way back to 1957.

Founded by Mr. Nagasamy Naidu under the name Anandha Vilas Biriyani Hotel in Dindigul.

He always wore a turban called THALAPA(a traditional head dress), which over the years became synonymous with his brand and cooking styles…
